About Ficho

Filmmaker, fixer & production problem-solver.

I’m Filip “Ficho” Zafran, a Berlin-based filmmaker and production all-rounder. Over the years I’ve worked as an assistant director, location scout/manager, production coordinator, transport and production manager, producer and casting producer – supporting feature films, commercials, TV work and short films across Europe and beyond.

I enjoy being in the space where logistics and creativity meet: keeping sets running smoothly, anticipating problems before they appear, and giving directors and clients the freedom to focus on performance and story. Whether it’s finding the right street in the right light, coordinating international shoots, or holding the schedule together on chaotic days, I like making complex productions feel simple from the outside.
