About Ficho

Filmmaker, fixer, and production problem-solver.

I'm Filip "Ficho" Zafran — filmmaker, location specialist and production all-rounder with roots in Croatia and a base in Berlin. My background spans across Assistant Directing, Location Scouting / Management, Production Coordination and Commercial Production.

I’ve worked on international feature films, European productions, TVS's, and branded content.

My focus lies in: managing call sheets, running set operations, scouting the right locations, and keeping departments aligned under pressure. I’m happiest when things are moving fast.

I woork well in multicultural teams and thrive in dynamic environments, adapting quickly to changing circumstances. I work both in English and German, as well as my native Croatian. I have worked across Europe and Africa, including Germany, Croatia, Slovenia, Slovakia, Bosnia, and Kenya and have very high cultural awareness.

In addition, I am also a have a background in web developing and my IT knowledge often comes in handy when dealing with digital workflows planning and bidding. I use my IT skills to improve efficiency and reduce costs on productions whenever possible.
